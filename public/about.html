Experiences and learnings
For a more professional version, see my resume.
I believe that different aspects of my personality and experiences, ever since I was a toddler have had an impact on my professional interests and the person I shape up to be. As a child, I was always fascinated by books and loved reading every night before bed. It started with the story of the three little pigs, but soon I was delving into magical worlds created by authors like Roald Dahl and J.K. Rowling. Reading these books helped me build my imagination skills and sparked my interest in storytelling.

In addition to reading, I also enjoyed sketching and painting. I took drawing classes until the 10th grade, with a particular interest in portraits. This helped me develop my observational skills and attention to detail. Academically, I've always been fond of numbers and loved problem-solving. My love for technology started when my uncle gifted me an iPod, and I was amazed at how it functioned. By the age of 15, I was obsessed with all things tech and spent hours on YouTube watching videos about the latest products in the tech space. It was during this time that I stumbled upon Steve Jobs‚Äô biography in my school library, and it had a profound impact on me. By the age of 17, I was convinced that I wanted to pursue computer science engineering at IIIT Delhi for my bachelor's degree.

During my first semester, I took a course called Prototyping Interactive Systems, taught by Dr. Aman Parnami, which taught me how to evaluate designs critically, envisioning them as part of our daily lives. As part of the course project, I also worked on building a smart wardobe called Aba. It was during a hackathon, while working on the front-end of our project, that I discovered the beautiful world of dribbble and behance, which inspired me to learn more about UI and UX design. Following that, I also took some more design courses in my second year and took up a research project under Dr. Grace Eden. Somewhere in between, I knew I wanted to pursue a career in this field.

Through my time at Upstox, Openhouse and Proctur, I've had the chance to work with some very talented people on some very exciting projects. I've learned a lot about the nuances of design and building a product as part of a team. These experiences along with my interests and experiences, have shaped me into the digital product designer that I am today. I'm excited about what the future holds and can't wait to see where this journey takes me next!
