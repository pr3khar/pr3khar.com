learnings & experiences
In 2019, I joined IIIT Delhi to pursue my bachelors in Computer Science. During my first semester, I took a course called Prototyping Interactive Systems, taught by Dr. Aman Parnami, which taught me how to evaluate designs critically, envisioning them as part of our daily lives. As part of the course project, I also worked on building a smart wardobe called Aba. Following that, I also took some more design courses in my second year and took up a research project under Dr. Grace Eden. Somewhere in between, I knew I wanted to pursue a career in this field.

My professional journey began at an Ed-tech startup called Proctur. Looking back, I'm not particularly proud of my early designs, but I'll never forget the thrill of seeing something I created go live and be used by real people. My next step took me to Upstox, a leading trading platform in India, where I gained invaluable experience in designing at scale and collaborating with so many talented individuals. Next, Openhouse offered a completely different experience as the solo designer, allowing me to build exciting 0-1 projects while closely collaborating with engineering and product teams to meet stakeholder expectations. Currently, I'm immersed in designing meaningful experiences at Crib.
