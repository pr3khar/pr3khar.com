Crib
Designing a Property Rental Management app for India
Crib is a property management software that helps rental businesses streamline their finance, operations and management. It helps in automatic rent reminders, tenant KYC, digital agreement creation & signing, finance management, complaint management, beds inventory management among many others among others. Crib is currently used by over 1000 businesses managing 1 lakh+ beds across 15+ cities in India.

Revamping the home screen of
Increased conversion rate by X2, converting twice as many enterprise customers.
The initial version (v1) of our business app's home screen was quite functional but lacked excitement. Feedback from one of our investors highlighted the importance of our home screen in clearly representing the essence of our app, akin to how Zomato signifies food or MakeMyTrip embodies travel.

The first impression of our app is pretty crucial in driving sales conversion, so it was imperative to enhance its appeal. It was crucial to gain a deep understanding of our customers, i.e., property owners to achieve this, so I accompanied the sales team on some visits. This helped me understand their world, comprehend their language, and grasp their persona more intimately.

In my conversations with property owners, a recurring theme emerged: their enthusiasm for discussing the quantity of properties they owned or the number of tenants under their management and likewise. These figures were a source of genuine pride for them. Recognizing this, we realized that showcasing these numbers prominently, accompanied by appealing visuals, could help them connect better to our application and add a delightful touch to the experience, showcasing that yes, we understand them.
Further changes were focused on enriching the infographics to provide more comprehensive information. Previously, we only highlighted the amount due for a specific period. However, we recognized the value in presenting a fuller picture, including total earnings, outstanding dues, and collected amounts. We also reduced the number of data points on the home screen to make it more focused on the integral elements of the business.

Additionally, we introduced a new section to showcase the latest features added in each update accordingly.

#3
Simplifying recording of payments
Increased engagement by 40%
Initially, we assumed that once customers began managing their business with Crib, they would transition to the online payment system, streamlining their operations. However, we discovered that the industry still heavily relies on cash transactions. Therefore, users had to manually mark payments received in the Crib app. The old payment recording process was too lengthy, causing users to abandon it altogether, leading to disengagement. Thus, our goal became simplifying the process to boost engagement.
The old flow
Insights
After asking and observing users, I noticed a few key points. Many property owners delegate tasks to their managers and staff, many of them weren't very comfortable with English, leading to reluctance in using the app. Although owners want their staff to use it to prevent leaks, they struggle to enforce this. Another insight is that some users don't record transactions immediately upon receipt but rather at the end of the day, logging all cash collected simultaneously.
Adding vocal coachmarks for integral flows
The updated experience
How did we make it better?
Making the entire app multi-lingual would have required significant bandwidth, so we thought of vocal coachmarks, which play twice per User ID in their chosen language, guiding users through critical flows effectively.

Additionally, we streamlined the payment recording process into a single bottom sheet, significantly reducing the time needed to record a payment. As a result, we observed a remarkable 40% increase in the number of payments recorded within the following month.

#4
RentQR: Making payment management simple
Adoption rate >50% in the first month
Most of our small and medium-sized business (SMB) operators prefer accepting online payments through UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm via QR codes). This is because they can easily replace their QR codes and receive payments in different bank accounts linked to these codes. However, this process requires operators to manually mark transactions as paid on Crib to keep their finances organized. Even with our simplified flow, this manual task remains burdensome. When operators' finances become unmanageable on the app, they often stop using Crib altogether due to the overwhelming workload, which was a big concern.

#5
Building Crib+
Significantly boosted the conversion of enterprise customers
What is Crib+?
Large enterprise customers prefer not to utilize the Crib app due to concerns about losing their brand recognition when the tenant app is labeled under Crib's name. While they appreciate our product and features, they desire a solution that aligns more closely with their branding. That's where Crib+ comes in. With Crib+, large enterprise customers can now enjoy their own personalized app for tenants, complete with their brand name and colors. This not only helps them maintain their brand identity but also streamlines operations using our technology and product offerings.
